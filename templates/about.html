Sara Sweets is making cakes and cupcakes since 2015 when I discovered my passion for Bakery.

At the beginning I baked my cakes and cupcakes for friends and family events, they suggested me to sell my sweets but I wasn't too sure if it would work for me to spend that time, money and effort without big clients, neither website nor local-based. But, my friends and family were extended mouth by mouth how good my sweets were and step by step I was increasing the orders and clients.

Slowly, slowly I was improving my skills on bakery and the challenges become easier, especially with the fancy cakes, and colorful cupcakes and I was so proud how my sweets looked like the more professional finish.

I would like to share with you how I met my main client:
One day, my parents and I decided to explore alittle bit more the city I used to live, and we stopped in a coffee shop to treat ourselves. They asked for a coffee and juice, and I asked for a tea and a piece of cake. When we finished and we were going to pay, the owner of the coffee shop asked me for my honest feedback about the cake. That piece of cake wasn't the best I tried in my short life. I told him that "If someone how knows a little bit about cakes try that one, it would tell him was really bad".
After this he asked me if I was a professional bakery and I told him "I'll bring some examples in a couple of days and you just need to give as complementary for getting some feedback, once you finish all of them call me and let me know the feedback and what you think about it".
One week after I had his first order and every week after this I was baking for him 4 big cakes per week for selling on his coffee shop even his clients were ordering cakes for their special occasions and the dream becomes true.

I was running my own bakery company!
